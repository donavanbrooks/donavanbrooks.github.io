Career Objectives

After graduating I will be moving to Minneapolis and working as an Entry-Level Software Engineer at C.H Robinson. It handles everything from tracking global shipments, data analytics and connection with customers. It is platform used by large organizations, such as Amazon and Walmart, to mom and pops with a single truck. I plan on working there for a couple years while I develop my own music platform and further develop my senior design project. I eventually want to take a different position and move to New York, working while trying to get my own startup off the ground.
